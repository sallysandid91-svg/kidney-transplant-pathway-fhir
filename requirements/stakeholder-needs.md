# Stakeholder Needs

## Nephrologist

- View patient transplant history
- Access laboratory results
- Monitor kidney function over time

## Transplant Center

- Manage transplant waiting list
- Record transplant procedures
- Track post-transplant outcomes

## Laboratory

- Provide standardized laboratory results
- Use LOINC coded observations

## Patient

- Ensure continuity of care
- Maintain longitudinal health records
