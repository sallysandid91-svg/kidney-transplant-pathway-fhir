# Kidney Transplant Care Pathway

## Objective

Represent a kidney transplant patient journey using HL7 FHIR resources and healthcare terminologies.

## Actors

- Patient
- Nephrologist
- Laboratory
- Transplant Center

## Workflow

1. Initial nephrology consultation
2. Chronic kidney disease diagnosis
3. Laboratory investigations
4. Waiting list registration
5. Kidney transplantation
6. Post-transplant follow-up

## Terminologies

### SNOMED CT

709044004 - Chronic kidney disease
